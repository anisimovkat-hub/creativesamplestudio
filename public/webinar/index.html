About the webinar

Launching a brand is about far more than designing a collection.

Many founders spend months perfecting their product, only to discover that success depends on much more than great design. Brand positioning, commercial strategy, product development, production planning and market entry all have to work together — not in isolation — for a brand to be commercially sustainable.

In this free session, you'll discover the strategic framework behind successful fashion brand launches, and why the strongest brands are built as systems. Drawing on real-world industry experience, we'll unpack the most common challenges founders face, the mistakes that quietly delay growth, and the key decisions that need to be made before investing in development and production.

Your speakers

Meet the Expert Panel

Evgeniya Khorosheva Co-Founder & CEO | Fashion Business & Product Development Strategist

Evgeniya is the Co-Founder and CEO of Creative Sample Studio (CSS), a London-based fashion consultancy helping startups, emerging brands and established businesses build commercially successful fashion brands. With over 16 years of experience across manufacturing facilities, ateliers and fashion brands, and more than seven years leading CSS, she combines strategic business thinking with hands-on expertise in product development and production. During this webinar, she will share the strategic framework behind successful fashion brand launches and explain why sustainable brands are built as systems — not just collections.

Ksenia McGinn Brand & Market Strategy Consultant

Ksenia is a Brand Strategist with over 12 years of experience in marketing and brand strategy, including eight years at Google UK. Her expertise lies in brand positioning, customer strategy and market differentiation. During this webinar, Ksenia will explain how strategic positioning, branding and market entry decisions influence long-term growth and commercial success.

Ludovica Ferrari Commercial Fashion Strategist

Ludovica is a senior fashion executive with more than 10 years of international experience across luxury fashion and lifestyle brands, specialising in commercial strategy, wholesale, international expansion and business operations. During this webinar, Ludovica will share practical insights into pricing, profitability, assortment planning and distribution strategy.

About Creative Sample Studio

CSS bridges the gap between a great idea and a commercially viable brand.

Founded in 2018, CSS bridges the gap between creativity and commercial success — recognising that many talented founders had strong ideas, but lacked the strategic, technical and production expertise needed to bring them to market.
